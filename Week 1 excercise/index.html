Dwyane Wade

Father Prime

Dwyane Wade is without a doubt, one of the most recognizable names in the NBA today. 'Father Prime' as his ex-teammates in Miami would call him, has put together one of the most illustrious careers the league has ever seen. Here's a brief outline of some of D-Wade's top achievements:

  • 2006 NBA Finals MVP
  • 2009 NBA scoring champion
  • 3x NBA Champion
  • 2008 Olympic Gold Medalist
  • 12x Allstar
